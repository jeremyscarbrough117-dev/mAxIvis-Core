mAxIvis.AI: Human-Augmentation for the Trades

The Mission

mAxIvis.AI is a "Power Couple" enterprise dedicated to solving human problems in the trades (HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical). We use AI to give human workers a "Savage" edge, not to replace them.

Core Modules

IDVS (Intelligent Dispatch & Verification System): AI-powered safety and quality auditing.

Vera-Hire: A talent marketplace for certified "Savage" technicians.

Apprentice Log: A nationwide digital affidavit system for General Contractor licensing.

Ivis-Sync: Global dispatch and telemetry managed by Ivis Daniela Vera Sanchez.
